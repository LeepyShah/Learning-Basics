FORT DHARIYAWAD WELCOMES YOU!

Converted into a heritage hotel, the elegant Fort of Dhariyawad holds a glorious history.
Located at the south of Rajasthan, Fort Dhariyawad was founded by the Royal Prince Sahasmal (the second son of the legendary hero Maharana Pratap of Mewar, Udaipur) in the middle of 16th century.

Our Attractions

Sitamata Sanctuary

Bestowed with rich biodiversity, Sitamata Sanctuary is a wilderness pilgrimage, famous for the rare flying squirrels and leopards.

Riverside Jungle Lunch

Relish a uniquely tranquil lunch experience right in the core of the jungle alongside a perennial river.

Jeep Safari

People enjoy to visit the area around the hotel and sanctuary by Jeep.

Naglia Dam

It is a small dam designed to reduce flow velocity which also controls flooding in that area.

Jakham Dam

This dam is years old. It was constructed to confine the reservoir to permit a higher water elevation and storage

Tribal Village Tour

The city of Dhariyawad is enclosed with a number of small villages with tribes such as Bhil and Meena constituting the population.


They stay in huts composed of mud and straw with thatched roofs and floors coated with cow dung and mud. The villagers make handcrafted doors and create designs on them using wheat starch.

Evening Safari With High Tea

The Evening Safari at Fort Dhariyawad is a thrilling and rejuvenating experience. The excursion begins with a drive into the forest of Sitamata Sanctuary followed by high tea.
